Welcome to Ramapuram Corporate Fellowship (RCF)

We are a community of professionals in Ramapuram, Chennai, devoted to honoring God, growing in faith, and making disciples through regular meetings, fellowship, and service. Join us to connect, learn, and serve together.

Volunteer With Us.

As RCF Fellowship, we visit under‑privileged villages to share the Good News and to provide practical support — supplying basic necessities and food items to families in need. Join our outreach teams to serve, encourage, and make a lasting difference in the community.

I'm Interested

Join a Connect Group.

Join a Connect Group to grow in faith, build lasting friendships, and support one another. Our small groups meet weekly across Ramapuram for Bible study, prayer, discussion, and practical outreach. Groups are welcoming to newcomers and designed for busy professionals, young adults, families, and those who prefer online meetings.
