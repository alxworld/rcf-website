We Are Ramapuram Corporate Fellowship (RCF)

Ramapuram Corporate Fellowship (RCF) is a community of believers in Ramapuram, Chennai, committed to honouring God and making disciples in the workplace, neighbourhoods and families. We are a diverse community where people grow in faith, serve together, and practically live out the love of Christ.

Our Purpose

We exist to glorify God by helping people encounter Jesus, grow in spiritual maturity, and engage their workplaces and communities with the gospel. We disciple believers, equip leaders, and cultivate a culture of prayer, service and gospel witness.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To make disciples who bring Kingdom values into their workplaces and homes.
Vision: A growing fellowship of Christ-centered followers who influence Chennai and beyond through transformed lives and compassionate service.

Our Core Values

Christ-centered

Jesus Christ is at the centre of our teaching, worship and mission.

Discipleship

We invest in spiritual growth through Bible teaching, mentoring and small groups.

Community

We practise authentic relationships and mutual care—church as family.

Service

We serve our city and neighbors, demonstrating God’s love in practical ways.


What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is God’s authoritative word and the foundation for faith and life.

God & Trinity

We believe in one God revealed as Father, Son and Holy Spirit—three persons, one God.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, who died and rose again for our sins.

Life in the Spirit

We rely on the Holy Spirit for new life, spiritual growth and empowerment for service.


Ministries & Programs

Connect Groups

Small groups that meet regularly for Bible study, prayer and mutual care.

Tracts & Outreach

Distribution team that shares concise Gospel tracts across parks, campuses and public spaces—inviting people to encounter Christ.

Volunteer Teams

Opportunities to serve in hospitality, worship, media, and outreach.

Community Outreach

Regular outreach initiatives to support local families and neighbourhoods.
